Project Plan

My project that I would like to develop is a smartphone app that tracks all of the users paid subscriptions. When an individual subscribes to services such as Netflix, vpn’s, newspapers sometimes they forget that they are subscribed and so they are automatically paying a monthly bill without even knowing. This app aims to solve that. The app will be written in Java as that what we are currently learning, for IOS and android mobile phones and potentially even desktops.

A survey conducted showed that more than 70% of users forget to cancel their recurring subscription bill and end up paying for it. And interestingly 29% did not know that it was recurring and that could be a feature for my app, a monthly reminder that theres going to be recurring charges. (Source: http://www.marketwired.com/press-release/hiatus-survey-62-consumers-waste-money-on-unwanted-subscriptions-because-they-dont-cancel-2107639.htm)

The way my app would work is that after a user has subscribed to a service (we will use Netflix as an example) they can enter in the details of the subscription eg Name of service, Start date, bill type (recurring or not), price, total cost for a year. After they enter the details into the app, the app will automatically remind them when the recurring charge will incur 1 days prior to give them time to either cancel or keep the service, and will also keep track of how much the user has and is spending on the service to give them a good idea of how they’re managing their money.

For this project we will only need just a piece of software and hardware. For the software since we will be coding in java we need the latest version of eclipse (https://www.eclipse.org) and a phone, ill be using my personal phone (iPhone XS) and if needed to test on android can use a emulated phone in eclipse.

The skills required would be programming knowledge which would be the hardest tool to obtain since we already have the hardware, we are doing a programming subject which makes it easier but for the more advanced things we might have to consult the CSIT mentors and or the tutors.

If this program is successful it will achieve its goal of informing users on when their subscriptions are about to incur a monthly charge reminding them of it to save them money, and as I previously mentioned this is a real problem and one that I have experienced many times, a lot of people are losing money from it and this will definitely help.
